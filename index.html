JioAirFiber FAQ

What is JioAirFiber?

JioAirFiber is a service that will redefine how we experience and manage our homes and businesses. It provides an opportunity for TV or broadband users to upgrade to a world-class home entertainment, broadband, and digital experience, delivered through one integrated service. JioAirFiber provides the best-in-class value proposition and plans as JioFiber.

Digital Entertainment:

  1. Top 550+ digital TV channels. All your favourite TV channels are available in high-definition.
  2. Catch-up TV: You can now go back as long as you want and pull out your favourite shows to watch them later.
  3. The most popular 15+ OTT apps: JioAirFiber gives you free access to the leading OTT apps. You can also utilise this subscription to use the apps across any device of your choice, be it TV, laptop, mobile or tablet.

Broadband: With indoor Wi-Fi service, you enjoy Jio's reliable Wi-Fi connectivity and high-speed broadband experience everywhere in your home and business premises.


Smart Home Service: Enables the following smart systems at your home for an exceptional smart living experience.

  1. Cloud PC for education and work-from-home
  2. Security and surveillance solutions
  3. Healthcare
  4. Education
  5. Smart Home IoT
  6. Gaming
  7. Home networking

Home devices at no additional cost

  1. Latest Wi-Fi router for coverage everywhere in your home
  2. 4K smart set-top box
  3. Voice-active remote
I already have a JioFiber connection. Can I switch to JioAirFiber?

JioFiber provides the best-in-class service experience for connectivity and entertainment services. However, you can still opt for JioAirFiber as an additional high-quality backup service.

How to get a new JioAirFiber connection?

Step 1: Connect with us

  1. Give a missed call on 60008-60008 to book via WhatsApp
  2. Go to our website:https://www.jio.com/selfcare/interest/airfiber
  3. Visit your nearest Jio Store

Step 2: Complete the booking process

Register for JioAirFiber services in a few quick and easy steps online or offline.

Step 3: Get Confirmation

We will get in touch with you and connect your home on priority as soon as services are available in your locality.


How do I check if JioAirFiber is available in my city?

You can check the availability of JioAirFiber in your city in a few simple steps. Just click on this link https://www.jio.com/jcms/airfiber/ and check if your city's name is there.

What is the difference between JioAirFiber and JioFiber?

JioAirFiber will provide best-in-class value propositions and plans, just like JioFiber.

Is installation required for getting a new JioAirFiber connection? Is installation paid?

To ensure an ultra-fast connection, an outdoor unit will be installed on your terrace/rooftop or outside your home. This installation service is chargeable at Rs. 1,000 but will be waived off in case you opt for an annual plan. You can also take the benefit of credit/debit card-based EMI to pay monthly and still get the benefits of an annual plan.
